My Name is Joey Garcia, and I develop web applications.

This is where my sales pitch goes. Well, at least, this is the part where I try to sell you the idea of how different I am...until you realize why I worked in a warehouse and not in sales. I'm not that different. Check the resume to learn about the guy you want to interview, but this section is about the guy you'll chat with at the water cooler. Like most, I've spent a lot of time working becuase it pays the bills. I've learned a lot, met some amazing people, grown; but most importantly, I've moved on. Construction was fun. I've helped build houses, construct runway lighting, and learned how much electrical current the body can handle before regretting not going to college. Just kidding...I went to college. I enjoy working with people. A team-oriented work ethic has been a driving force in every aspect of my career. Connecting with individuals and forming relationships that foster collaboration is my approach to tackling large-scale problems that require creative solutions. I love to code. This industry is unique in that it is innately creative and often requires an unrivaled level of critical thinking. Whew, one does not appreciate a thesaurus until they find themselves writing an "about me" section. In any event, life's been great and it's time to start doing what I love for a living. Hopefully we can work together soon. I'll learn, you'll laugh, it'll be a great time. Kick back, look around, and enjoy the site. Welcome to my page!
